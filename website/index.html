Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about trading, security, fees, and verification on EthioSwap.

EthioSwap is a peer-to-peer (P2P) escrow platform that allows individuals in Ethiopia to buy and sell USD ($) directly from each other using local payment methods like CBE, Telebirr, and others. The platform acts as a secure escrow provider. When a trade starts, the seller's USD is automatically locked. Once the buyer sends the fiat payment and the seller confirms receipt, the USD is released to the buyer.

Yes. To ensure a safe, fraud-free environment and comply with international anti-money laundering (AML) standards, all users must complete identity verification (KYC) before they can initiate any buy or sell trades. You will need to fill out your details (name, age, phone) and upload clear pictures of your National ID or Passport (front and back) along with a live camera selfie. Our admins review submissions quickly to authorize accounts.

When a buyer responds to a sell offer, the seller's USD balance is moved into the EthioSwap secure Escrow holding vault. The seller cannot cancel the trade while in escrow, preventing them from running away with the assets after payment. The buyer is given 15 minutes to transfer the fiat money to the seller. Once paid, the buyer marks it as paid. The seller then verifies their bank statement and releases the escrow. If any issue arises, either party can open a dispute.

If a buyer claims they paid but the seller refuses to release, or if a buyer marks a trade as paid without transferring the funds, a dispute can be opened. Once a dispute is active, a certified EthioSwap admin will step into the secure trade chat room. Both users must provide verified screenshots or PDF receipts of the transaction. The admin will verify the logs and release the USD to the buyer or refund it to the seller based on audit evidence.

EthioSwap charges a highly competitive, transparent commission fee on every completed trade. The commission rate is set dynamically by the platform administrator (typically between 1.0% and 5.0%) and is shown clearly to you before you start a trade. Fees are deducted only upon successful trade completion. There are zero deposit fees, and withdrawal processing fees are calculated dynamically.
